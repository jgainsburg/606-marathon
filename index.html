⚙️ One-time Firebase setup (~5 minutes, free)

Right now sign-ups are only visible on your device. To make the page live and shared with everyone, follow these steps:

  1. Go to console.firebase.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
  2. Click Add project. Name it anything (e.g. "606marathon"). Skip Google Analytics. Click through to create it.
  3. On the project dashboard, click the </> (Web) icon. Name the app anything and click "Register app."
  4. You'll see a firebaseConfig block with your API key and project ID. Copy the whole thing.
  5. In the left sidebar, go to Build → Realtime Database. Click "Create database," choose the US server, and start in test mode (this allows public read/write -- fine for a personal event).
  6. Open 606Marathon_HTML_v3.html in any text editor. Near the top of the <script>, find FIREBASE_CONFIG and replace the placeholder values with your actual config.
  7. Save, reload -- the grid will now sync live across all your friends' devices.
